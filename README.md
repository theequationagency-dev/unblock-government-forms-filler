# Form Filler — Seminars & Events

A simple, offline app for filling out registration forms for seminars and events.
Save your details once and reuse them on any form, then print or save as PDF.
Built for when browser autofill isn't working — no internet, no install, no accounts.

## How to use it

1. **Open the app.** Double-click `index.html` — it opens in any web browser.
2. **My Info tab.** Enter your name, email, address, etc. once and click **Save my info**.
   Your details are stored only in your own browser on your device — nothing is sent anywhere.
3. **Fill a Form tab.** Pick a template (Seminar, Event, or General/Government).
   Your saved details are filled in automatically.
4. Add the event-specific details (event name, date, session), review everything,
   then click **Print / Save as PDF**. In the print dialog, choose *Save as PDF*
   to keep a copy you can email or bring with you.

## Privacy

All your information stays in your browser's local storage on this device.
The app makes no internet connection and sends no data to any server.

## Files

- `index.html` — the entire app (self-contained: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in one file).
