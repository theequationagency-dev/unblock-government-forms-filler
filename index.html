Everything is editable. Tap Open ↗ to go to the real sign-up page, then use the Copy buttons to paste each answer in — or Print / Save as PDF to keep a copy.

ℹ️ For your safety, this app can't type into another website's form (no site can fill another site's fields). So it fills a copy here — tap Copy on a field and paste it into the real form.

Tip: In the print dialog, choose “Save as PDF” as the destination to keep a copy.
